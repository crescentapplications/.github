# Crescent Applications

## Executive Summary

Crescent Applications is a software publisher based in Irvine, California, that develops purpose-driven digital products for education, community, commerce, and everyday life. Our portfolio includes Islamic educational applications, Arabic-first SaaS platforms, and mobile solutions that simplify work, shopping, and social experiences.

## Vision

We envision technology that strengthens communities, preserves cultural identity, expands knowledge, and enhances everyday life through meaningful and accessible digital experiences.
              
## Mission

Our mission is to develop ethical, mobile-first software that addresses real-world needs across education, work, commerce, and community life. We focus especially on Arabic-speaking audiences and culturally relevant user experiences that are often underserved by mainstream platforms.

## Offerings

Our offerings include Islamic jurisprudence and educational applications, Arabic-first workforce and classifieds platforms, eCommerce solutions, and social planning tools. We build products that combine intuitive design, bilingual accessibility, practical utility, and strong ethical foundations.

## Core Values

At Crescent Applications, our values guide the way we build products, collaborate, and serve our users.

1. Intention: Our primary intention is to please God.
2. Passion: We love what we do.
3. Integrity: Ethics and principles guide our actions.
4. Communication: We are committed to clear, effective, and transparent communication.
5. Synergy: We believe in the power of teamwork to achieve exceptional outcomes.
6. Planning: We plan for quality, scope (quantity), schedule (time), and cost (budget).
7. Kaizen: We strive for continuous improvement.
8. Simplicity: We solve complex problems by breaking them down into simpler ones.
9. Trust: We place our trust in God.
